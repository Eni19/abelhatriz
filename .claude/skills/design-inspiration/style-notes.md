# Style Notes

_Distilled from references/ — last updated after reviewing: Screenshot_1.png, Screenshot_2.png, everdell.jpg, wanderhome.jpg, wanderhome2.webp_

## Color palette
- Backgrounds lean warm and soft: cream/parchment (Screenshot_2, near-white with a warm tint) or full illustrated dusk/meadow gradients (Wanderhome: lavender → rose → gold sky over deep green/blue hills; Everdell: golden-hour forest greens).
- Deep forest green and mossy green show up constantly as a grounding dark tone (Everdell trees, Wanderhome hills).
- Accent colors are muted/dusty rather than saturated: dusty rose/mauve, muted gold or mustard (the pixel flower in Screenshot_2), soft lavender-blue.
- Dark UI elements (Screenshot_1's card, Screenshot_2's nav pill) are a warm near-black, not pure black.
- Overall contrast is gentle — this is a painterly, storybook palette, not a high-contrast tech palette.

## Typography
- Two distinct registers, always paired:
  1. A romantic/ornate display face for titles and logos — flourished calligraphic script (Wanderhome logo) or a refined classic serif (Screenshot_2's large headline, "Where starting up a real world company...").
  2. A quiet, modern sans-serif for body copy and UI chrome (Screenshot_1 and 2's small nav/body text) — small size, generous line height, low visual weight so it doesn't compete with the illustration/display type.
- Headline serif type is used sparingly and large; body sans is used for everything functional (nav, buttons, small print).

## Layout & spacing
- Generous whitespace even when the background is a busy illustration — UI elements (nav, cards) float in mostly-empty space rather than tiling edge to edge.
- Content is often centered or in a single clear focal column, not busy multi-column grids.
- Full-bleed illustrated backgrounds (Everdell, Wanderhome) contrasted with minimal, small floating UI chrome on top (Screenshot_1, 2) — the illustration carries the mood, the UI stays out of its way.

## Components
- Rounded-pill shapes dominate interactive chrome: pill-shaped nav bars, pill buttons ("Get Cofounder" in Screenshot_2), circular play buttons (Screenshot_2).
- Cards are softly rounded (large radius, not sharp corners), with soft drop shadows rather than hard borders — they read as "floating" over the background.
- Iconography leans hand-crafted/charming rather than corporate: pixel-art flower icon (Screenshot_2), painterly illustrated figures/creatures (Everdell, Wanderhome) rather than flat vector icon sets.

## Overall mood
Cottagecore/storybook fantasy warmth (Everdell, Wanderhome) crossed with a very calm, minimal modern product UI (Screenshot_1, 2). Think: a fairytale illustrated world, viewed through a clean, quiet, softly-rounded interface — romantic, nostalgic, gentle, unhurried. Good fit for anything themed around folklore, nature/botany, journeys, or handwritten-letter intimacy.
